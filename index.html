about slwy

A house party for everyone to hang out with good vibes, great food and live music. Come and connect with virtuous friends, new and old. Most of all - have fun.

music

The Odds, Heyday and friends will be performing live music to help us set the mood. Feel free to suggest songs that you want to hear during the event in the message section when registering for the event!

We're fortunate to have Shyan be the one planning for our food. If you would like to bring your own food as well, please do indicate when registering! We would love to be able to form a community that shares homemade food with one another.

We would like for slwy to be a platform for us to give back to BW monastery. We will be setting up a drinks bar where you can donate to obtain a drink of your own choice.
