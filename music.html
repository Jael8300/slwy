Music

The universal language that connects souls across cultures and generations. Join us as we explore the rhythms that move us and the melodies that define our shared experiences.

Hey Day

A passionate collective of youth who bond over their shared love for Eastern musical instruments, beautifully blending traditional sounds with modern keyboard accompaniment. Featuring the enchanting melodies of the guzheng and the soulful expressions of the erhu, Hey Day creates a unique musical experience that bridges ancient traditions with contemporary sensibilities.

@theheydayco

Yazers

A choir dedicated to touching hearts with hope and positivity through music. With their love and zest for the arts, Yazers explores various musical genres while pursuing excellence through diligent practice. They perform with passion to spread positive energy and nurture confident members who contribute meaningfully to society.

@_yazers_

The Odds

A visionary band that reimagines spiritual music through modern soundscapes, creating fresh perspectives in today's musical landscape. Through their shared experience of music, The Odds crafts sacred spaces where hearts can find wisdom, compassion, and strength—offering renewed zest and deeper understanding of life through their thoughtful musical expressions.

@theodds.col
